FTC Team Steam

We are a small robotics team participating in the yearly FTC competition, based out of Palo Alto, California. We build robots, and we love doing it!
If you are interested in participating in the competition, you can find FTC info from the organization's official website.

This Year's Competition

As of September 9th, 2017, the 2017-2018 game has been released: FIRST Relic Recovery. Multiple Game scoring methods are available within the two minute thirty second game. A video and team spreadsheet of the scoring methods can be found below. With the Indiana Jones theme and a series of cool new scoring methods, Team STEAM is ready to overcome to obstacles of the building phase and take out spirit to the competition.
